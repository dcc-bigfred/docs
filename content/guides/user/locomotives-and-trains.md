# Locomotives and trains

In BigFred, a **vehicle** is a single unit in your catalogue — a locomotive, wagon,
EMU, driving trailer, or trolley. A **train** is a named group of vehicles that run
and are controlled **as one consist**.

A locomotive or wagon saved only to your catalogue does **not** run on a layout yet.
You must add it to the layout on its own, or as a member of a train that has been
added to the layout.

## Why define trains?

- **One throttle** — when driving a train, every powered vehicle in the consist moves
  together at the same speed and direction.
- **One function screen** — on the throttle cockpit you see functions (F0, F1, …) from
  locomotives and wagons at once; for example, you can turn on wagon lighting and
  locomotive sound without switching between vehicles.
- **One unit on the layout** — lend, return, and select a train in throttle mode as a
  single object instead of each wagon separately.

## Adding a locomotive or wagon

1. Open the **menu** (top-left icon) → **My** → **Vehicles**.
2. Click **Add vehicle**.
3. Enter a **name**, choose a **vehicle kind** (e.g. locomotive, wagon), and — if the
   vehicle should run on track — a **DCC address** from your assigned pool.
4. Confirm with **Add**.

Without a DCC address you can create a dummy vehicle (e.g. a wagon visible in a
consist but not drivable).

To run a vehicle **on its own** on a layout, use **Add to layout** on the **My
vehicles** list.

## Adding a vehicle to an existing train

1. **Menu** → **My** → **Trains**.
2. Click **Edit** on the train you want.
3. At the bottom of the form, pick a vehicle from the list and click **Add vehicle**.
4. Set the order with the **Up** / **Down** arrows; tick **reversed** if a wagon is
   coupled backwards.
5. Save with **Save**.

The vehicle must already exist under **My vehicles**. It cannot belong to two trains
at the same time.

## Creating a new train

1. **Menu** → **My** → **Trains**.
2. Click **Add train**.
3. Enter a **train name**.
4. Add at least one vehicle (**Add vehicle**), set the order, and mark any **reversed**
   couplings.
5. Confirm with **Add**.

Then use **Add to layout** on the train list so the consist appears on your layout
and is available under **Throttle**.
